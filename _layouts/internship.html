Overview

The internship program is conceived as an online summer internship and mentorship initiative, aimed primarily at Bangladeshi undergraduate and masters students who want structured exposure to advanced topics and research practice.

Projects may take the form of guided reading, problem-based skill development, computational exploration, or more research-oriented work depending on the mentor, the background of the student, and the maturity of the yearly call.

Program Structure

  • Typical duration: around 2 to 3 months
  • Meeting frequency: weekly or biweekly
  • Format: reading course, problem-based preparation, or research-oriented project
  • Mentors may include PhD students, postdoctoral researchers, or faculty

Why This Matters

The program is intended to help students move from coursework into sustained research habits. It creates an environment in which regular meetings, reading plans, and exposure to current topics can begin to build a more durable research culture.

For some students, the experience may support later thesis work, advanced coursework, or deeper research collaborations.

How To Apply

  • Check the yearly call and project list
  • Review expected background and prerequisites
  • Prepare a short statement of interest and academic background
  • Submit before the listed deadline once applications open
